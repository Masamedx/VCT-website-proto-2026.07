About VCT

What is Virtual Clinical Training?

Virtual Clinical Training (VCT) is an online continuing dental education (CPD) initiative that delivers interactive, specialist-led clinical sessions via Google Meet. We make high-quality, case-based learning accessible to dental practitioners and students across Malaysia and beyond — with every session recorded for registered participants.

Our mission

Closing the gap in clinical education

Access to structured, high-quality clinical education remains uneven across Malaysia — particularly for practitioners outside major urban centres such as Kuala Lumpur. VCT exists to make consistent, practical, expert-led learning available to every dentist, wherever they practise.

The challenges dentists face

  • Significant time constraints due to clinical workload
  • Travel limitations, including cost and logistical challenges
  • Limited access to consistent, high-quality lectures and mentorship

Why typical online sessions fall short

  • Often used mainly as promotional tools for onsite workshops
  • Frequently lacking in clinical depth and practical relevance
  • Minimal interaction or case-based learning

Our approach

A structured bridge between theory and practice

VCT is a structured online clinical series designed to bridge the gap between theoretical learning and hands-on training. It is not a replacement for onsite workshops — it is a strategic complement that prepares you beforehand and strengthens the overall learning ecosystem.

  • Practical, real-world clinical knowledge you can apply in daily practice
  • Less reliance on frequent travel to major cities for quality education
  • A learning bridge before hands-on workshops and advanced courses
  • Accessible education nationwide and internationally, including underserved regions

Multi-specialty

One platform, every discipline

In daily practice, patient care is rarely confined to a single discipline. VCT brings expertise from across dentistry into one learning ecosystem — helping you develop a holistic understanding of case management, improve interdisciplinary decision-making, and recognise when to treat, refer, or co-manage.

  • Periodontics
  • Implant Dentistry
  • Endodontics
  • Prosthodontics
  • Oral Surgery
  • Orthodontics & Clear Aligner
  • Paediatric Dentistry
  • Restorative & Aesthetic Dentistry
