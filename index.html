What is VCT?

Virtual Clinical Training (VCT) is an online continuing dental education (CPD) initiative delivering interactive, specialist-led clinical sessions via Google Meet — with every session recorded for registered participants.

  • Specialist-led & evidence-based — every session is taught by a specialist in a different field of dentistry.
  • Credentialed instructors — clinicians qualified through bodies such as the RCSEd and Universiti Malaya.
  • Live on Google Meet, recordings included — join from anywhere and revisit anytime.
